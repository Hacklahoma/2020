FAQ

Who can attend?

Any student over the age of 18 can participate, regardless of major, background, or skill level.

Does it cost anything?

Admissions is completely free! Everything is on us. That means meals, drinks, snacks, sleeping areas, and swag are all taken care of. Just bring yourself and your cool ideas!

Any travel reimbursements?

Yes! We will be reimbursing up to $50 for those who keep their phyiscal receipts and submit a project.

When are projects due?

Devpost submissions for projects are due at 11:30 AM, but you are allowed to continue working on your project until 12PM. If your Devpost submission is not turned in at 11:30, your project will not be eligible for judging.

Do I need any experience?

No experience is needed. Whether you're a coder, an artist, or a writer, you'll get to work with various mentors, attend workshops, interact with companies, and learn alongside fellow participants.

Are there teams?

We encourage everyone to work with a team! Teams may contain up to 4 people. We will also be offering a team-building session at the beginning of the hacking period.

What should I bring?

To check in, you must show your Student ID. You should also bring a laptop, chargers, toiletries, a change of clothes, sleeping bag, pillow, and anything else you would need for an overnight weekend. Keep in mind that Hacklahoma will last for 24hrs.

What is the Code of Conduct?

Hacklahoma welcomes students from all backgrounds and values the importance of a safe and all-inclusive space. Anyone attending must adhere to the MLH Code of Conduct.

Isn't hacking illegal?

Yes, hacking is illegal. We define a hack as jury-rigging something inelegantly but effectively, usually as a temporary solution to a problem. Like duct taping a hole in a sinking boat to keep it afloat.

Will there be food?

We will supply food for Saturday’s breakfast, lunch, dinner, and Sunday’s breakfast with plenty of snacks and drinks throughout. All free of charge!

Can I start my project before?

No, you cannot work or copy past projects. You can brainstorm ideas and collect whatever software and tools you need, as long as the project is completely new.

Do I need to stay for 24hrs?

No, you’re not confined here. Feel free to go home and get some rest, but be back in time for judging!

Prizes

First Place

This prize will be given to the team or individual who places first, overall. Each team member will recieve a Nintendo Switch Light, and Pokemon Shield OR Pokemon Sword.

Second Place

This prize will be given to the team or individual who places second, overall. Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Holy Stone Quadcopter Drone.

Third Place

This prize will be given to the team or individual who places third, overall. Each team member of the winning team will recieve Powerbeats3 Wireless Headphones.

Best Hardware

This category rewards the best overall hardware hack. To be considered for this prize, your hack must be associated with phyiscal hardware. Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Raspberry Pi 4.

Best Design

This category rewards the best overall designed hack. Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Razor Gaming Mouse and Creative Pebble USB Speakers.

Best Beginner

This category rewards the best beginner hack. To be considered for this prize, the hack must be created by first time hackers. Each team member of the winning team will recieve 2x Tile Mates and 2x Tile Slims.

Best STEM Education Hack

This category gives you the freedom to tackle any hack that is STEM Education related. Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Amazon Fire TV Stick and a $200 donation to a STEM charity of your choice.

Best use of Google Cloud

Build your hack with a suite of secure storage, powerful compute, and integrated data analytics products provided by Google Cloud to be eligible for this prize. Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Google Home Mini.

Best Domain Registered with Domain.com

Domain.com provides all the domain services you need from registration to hosting to SSL certificates and beyond. Create the best domain and be chosen to win! Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Domain.com Backpack.

Best UiPath Automation Hack

Build an automation hack using UiPath! Each winning team member will receive a UiPath/MLH Branded Backpack and an opportunity to interview for a UiPath Internship in 2020 with high recommendation! Each team member of the winning team will recieve a UiPath/MLH Backpack and be able to skip the Internship line with UiPath.

Best use of MongoDB Atlas

Create a hack using MongoDB Atlas to host a database in the cloud! Automate the provisioning and deployment process with ease, while focusing all your efforts on your hack. Each team member of the winning team will recieve a Branded MongoDB AirCharge Plus.
